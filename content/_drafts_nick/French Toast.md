# French Toast

From Kellyn Mendez's Aunt?

## Ingredients

- [ ] Bread, in 1" slices
- [ ] 1/2 cup sugar
- [ ] 1 tsp vanilla
- [ ] 1 cup milk
- [ ] cinnamon

## Procedure

1. Mix everything but bread in a bowl.
2. Dip bread in mixture.
3. Cook bread in butter on a hot pan til brown on both sides.
