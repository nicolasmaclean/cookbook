From Elisa Maclean

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1/4 cup flour
- [ ] 1 3/4 cup chicken broth
- [ ] 1/2 cup milk
- [ ] 1 chopped onion
- [ ] A rotisserie chicken
- [ ] Vegetables
- [ ] Spices (Curry, Paprika, ...)

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 425F.
1. Mix ingredients over medium heat.
1. Fill pie crust and bake for 35 minutes.
